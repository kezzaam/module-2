A little bit about me

I’m the creator of Moondog Design + Illustration, based in Nelson, New Zealand. I have a diverse employment background, from being a chef to working on boats and running my own business on the side. I have a solid foundation in the Adobe Creative Suite software. As well as graphic, web, and surface pattern design, I specialise in detailed vector illustration and teach online classes on Skillshare. My surface pattern design work combines over a decade of experience in graphic design with my passion for illustration and the natural environment. My first pattern collection, Rata Forest, was selected as a finalist in the NZ Bolt of Cloth 2020 Textile Design Award and I've since gone on to license patterns with NZ brands Sassypants, Honeywrap, Biopak and Woolbabe. I am a lifelong learner, currently studying software engineering part-time. Looking forward, I would love to start designing games as well as doing more animation and character design.
